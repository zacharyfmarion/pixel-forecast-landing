Privacy

Updated January 2026

Overview

Pixel Forecast is committed to your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Location Data

When you grant permission, we access your location to show local weather. This data is:

  • Used only to fetch weather
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never sold or shared

Third Parties

We use these services:

  • Apple WeatherKit - Weather data
  • Open-Meteo - City search
  • Sentry - Crash reporting (see below)

Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability. This includes:

  • Error messages and stack traces
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Navigation breadcrumbs (which screens you visited before a crash)

This data is used solely for debugging and does not include personal identifiers like your name or email. See Sentry's privacy policy.

No Advertising or Analytics

We do not use advertising networks, behavioral analytics, or user tracking beyond crash reporting.
